Few tips for you:

Replace CHANGEME in the url with something you will remember, then bookmark it.
Go to the bookmarked page when you want to track your boss kills, for example: www.robt.heroku.com/#/CYBERtown
Click the refresh icon on the monster sprite to (re)start the countdown.
Click the 'star' to pin a card.
Your data is automatically saved to the server.
Cards are sorted first by pinned, then time remaining, followed by monster name.
Click on the monster name or map to view their details.
At the moment there is only Pre-Renewal times available.
